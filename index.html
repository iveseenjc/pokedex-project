PROJECT EXPLANATION

  • The project centers around the Text-to-Speech API provided by Voice RSS.
  • While the project is themed around Pokémon, it only serves to make the site appealing and to have access to large amounts of data using the Poké API for the text-to-speech to use.
  • To interact with the website, pick a Pokémon on the right-most panel to reveal its data. You then click the "Evaluate Pokémon" button at the bottom panel and the text-to-speech will read out the Pokémon's description.
  • Voice RSS provides a variety of voices to choose from in different genders and accents. To select a voice, just click on the dropdown at the bottom panel, and select the name of your voice and it will change respectively.
  • NOTE: There might be slight delays when it comes to using the Voice API, so with that, we made it so that the button of the prompt will turn green to let you know that the Text-to-Speech is being processed. The data is retrieved from APIs after all and might also rely on the strength of your internet connection.
